Growatt 3kw inverter load-shedding kit:

This solution consists of a Growatt 3kw hybrid inverter with a Growatt 3.3kwh Lithium Ion battery to power your lights, essential plugs, fridges etc. It’s the perfect solution to beat load shedding with the option of adding solar to supply some of the load later.

Growatt is one of the most popular inverters on the market, with great after sales support and a user friendly mobile application to monitor things like battery power available, load being used, and Eskom power being used. The technical details:

  • 3KW AC output
  • 48V battery – lithium ion or deep cycle
  • 2.4kw solar capability
  • 3.3kwh battery capacity (upgradeable)

Deye 5KW inverter load-shedding kit:

This option will help you beat load-shedding with plenty room for expansion. With an impressive 5kw ac output, you can power just about all the lights in an average sized home, along with your essential plugs. The system includes a state of the art Deye 5kw Smart Hybrid inverter, Pylon Tech 3.5kwh lithium ion battery and installation with an electrical C.O.C.

The major advantage of this system is the dual AC output. When you add panels to this solution, the inverter will power most of your house using solar energy, using Eskom power only for the remaining load. While the system comes with a smartphone app to monitor the battery status, load status, and solar status. The technical details:

  • 5KW dual AC output
  • 48V Battery – Lithium Ion or deep cycle
  • 6.5KW solar capability
  • 3.5kwh battery capacity (upgradeable)
