Solar solutions for home or business.

South Africa has a serious problem with electricity. We have load shedding, and when we do have power it’s expensive. The solution lies in solar. More importantly, a tailor made, professionally installed solar system by Protégé Power.

Although our offices are based in Pretoria East, we operate anywhere in Gauteng and neighbouring provinces.

Not only is solar energy beneficial for your wallet, but it’s also kinder to our precious planet.

We can offer you a solution to suit just about any application and match your budget. With Eskom deteriorating at a consistent pace. So look no further than Protégé Power to keep the lights on!

We are a sister company of Protégé Protection. Which means we carry the same values and strict standards. We design and install top quality renewable energy solutions using only the best components available. However, we still keep budget in mind. Our large, professional team, allows us to offer you the kind of after-sales support you deserve. After all, a system is only efficient if it is efficiently maintained. That’s why we have a strong focus on quality and stick by our strict standards.
